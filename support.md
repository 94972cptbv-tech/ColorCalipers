# Color Calipers Support

Thank you for using Color Calipers.

Color Calipers is a color harmony and color sampling app for iPhone and iPad. It helps users explore color relationships, sample colors from photos or the camera, build palettes, save palettes, and create paint reference cards.

## Common Support Topics

### Camera or Photo Sampling

Color Calipers uses the camera and photo picker only for local color sampling. If camera or photo access does not work, check your device settings and make sure Color Calipers has permission to access the camera or selected photos.

### Saved Palettes

Saved palettes are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, locally saved palettes may also be removed.

### Paint Store Color Cards

Paint matches and digital color previews are approximate. Color appearance can vary by display, lighting, sheen, paint base, material, and surface. Always verify final paint choices with a physical manufacturer chip or paint store sample.

Color Calipers does not generate paint-store mixing formulas or tint percentages. Paint stores create formulas using their own brand, base paint, tint system, and colorant machines.
