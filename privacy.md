# Color Calipers Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 31, 2026

Color Calipers respects your privacy. The app is designed to work locally on your device and does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

## Data Collection

Color Calipers does not collect personal data.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external data services.

## Camera and Photo Use

Color Calipers may ask for access to your camera or photo picker so you can sample colors from objects, clothing, walls, materials, artwork, branding references, or other visual sources.

Camera and photo access is used only for color sampling inside the app.

Color Calipers does not upload your photos or camera images to a server. The app does not sell, share, transmit, or store your source images outside the app’s local color-sampling workflow.

## Saved Palettes

Color Calipers allows you to save palettes for later use. Saved palettes are stored locally on your device.

Saved palettes may include palette names, notes, color values, selected use case, harmony settings, and related color-selection preferences. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted by the app.

## Paint Matches

Color Calipers may display approximate paint matches based on local app data. Paint matching is calculated inside the app and is not sent to an external service.

Paint matches and digital color previews are approximate. Color appearance can vary by display, lighting, sheen, paint base, material, and surface. Always verify final paint choices with a physical manufacturer chip or paint store sample.

Color Calipers does not generate paint-store mixing formulas or tint percentages.

## Children’s Privacy

Color Calipers does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if Color Calipers changes its features or data practices. Any updates will be posted on this page.
